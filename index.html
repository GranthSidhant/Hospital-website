Departments

Our medical centre is home to several specialized departments, each dedicated to providing top-notch care in their respective fields. Our teams of experienced professionals work collaboratively to ensure comprehensive care and optimal outcomes for our patients.

  • Cardiology
  • Neurology
  • Hepatology
  • Pediatrics
  • Eye Care

Cardiology

Our Cardiology department offers comprehensive heart care services, from diagnostic testing to advanced treatments and surgical interventions.

We are equipped with state-of-the-art technology and staffed by a team of expert cardiologists who are dedicated to providing personalized care for each patient. Whether you're dealing with heart disease, hypertension, or require cardiac surgery, our team is here to support you every step of the way.

Neurology

Our Neurology department specializes in diagnosing and treating disorders of the nervous system, including the brain, spinal cord, and peripheral nerves.

Our team of neurologists and neurosurgeons is committed to providing cutting-edge care for conditions such as epilepsy, stroke, multiple sclerosis, and other neurological disorders. We offer advanced diagnostic services, personalized treatment plans, and a multidisciplinary approach to ensure the best outcomes for our patients.

Hepatology

The Hepatology department focuses on the diagnosis and treatment of liver, gallbladder, and biliary diseases.

Our hepatologists are experts in managing conditions such as hepatitis, liver cirrhosis, liver cancer, and fatty liver disease. We provide comprehensive care, including liver biopsies, imaging studies, and the latest treatment options to help manage and improve liver health.

Pediatrics

Our Pediatrics department provides comprehensive healthcare for infants, children, and adolescents.

From routine check-ups and vaccinations to managing complex medical conditions, our pediatricians are dedicated to ensuring the health and well-being of your child. We offer a child-friendly environment and a team of compassionate professionals to support your child's growth and development.

Eye Care

Our Eye Care department offers a wide range of services to address all your vision and eye health needs.

We provide routine eye exams, treatment for eye diseases, and advanced surgical procedures. Our team of ophthalmologists is equipped with the latest technology to diagnose and treat conditions such as glaucoma, cataracts, macular degeneration, and more. We are committed to helping you achieve and maintain optimal eye health.
